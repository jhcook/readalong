## ADR‑003: Use Forced Alignment for Word‑Level Highlighting
Status: Accepted

Context
Accurate highlighting requires mapping spoken words to text.
Parents may paraphrase or skip words.

Decision
Use Whisper timestamps as the primary alignment source, with optional refinement via Gentle or Aeneas.

Consequences
Positive

Accurate word‑level sync

Tolerates natural speech

Works offline

Negative

Alignment tuning required

Edge cases with heavy paraphrasing
